    • JAMType



    Coordinators:

    Aditya Sathe    Number

    Harry    Number

    Gragery    Number

    • Duration of Event (expected): 0 hours

    • Description of Event:

      Just-A-Minute to prepare and speak out on any topic presented to you.

    Rules:

  • 1. The panel shall have 6 participants at a time.
  • 2. The panelists are to speak for one minute on a given subject (given on the spot), without "hesitation, repetition or deviation".
  • 3. Hesitation includes momentary pause, fumbling, tripping over words, riding a laugh
  • 4. Repetition of any word or phrase
  • 5. Deviation from the given subject, deviating from the English language as we know it, deviation from grammar as we understand it, deviating from the truth and logic (incoherence)
  • 6. Sudden change in gestures and pace of speaking
  • 7. Use of first-person pronouns like I, me or myself
  • 8. The JAM Master has the right to add any round-specific rule.
  • 9. Any deviation from the rules mentioned above is valid for objection.
  • 10. A panellist scores one point for making a correct challenge against whoever is speaking, or the speaker gets a point if the challenge is deemed incorrect.
  • 11. A player who makes a correct challenge takes over the subject for the remainder of the minute, or until he or she is successfully challenged.
  • 12. The person speaking when the 60 seconds expires also scores a point. An extra point is awarded if a panellist speaks for the entire minute without being challenged. The speaker to end the session scores 2 points
