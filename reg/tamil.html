  • Description of Event:

    A fun event completely based on the knowledge of Tamil words, songs and Tamil TV shows.

Rules:

  • Round1: Song Paadu
    - General Rules of Tamil Antakshari
    - No Elimination

  • Round2: Joint Adi
    - a Tamil letter will be given to the team and the participants can form any number of words using it as prefix & suffix of words.
    - Elimination based on marks awarded by the Juries.

  • Round 3: Channel Maathu
    - As the names of television channels are spelled out, team member must act like the people on screen in that channel say like a cartoon or newsreader or serial actor.
    - Elimination based on marks awarded by the Juries

  • Round 4: Scene Podu
    - A situation will be given to a team and participants should act the way like the personality specified/choosen.
    - Elimination based on marks awarded by the Juries

  • Final Round: Thaakurom Thookurom
    - A list of Tamil words will be given to 1 in a team, and the other should find out the words based on the clues given by the team member.

  • Tie Breaker: Forming words from given tamil letters

    • Description of Event:

      Maatri Pesavum, a turncoat debate. An event to prove your convincing ability, communication skills and spontaneous thinking, where the speaker has to speak for and against the motion when he/she hears the signal from the judges.
      Format: Prelims, Semi-finals and Finals.


    • Registration fees: Rs. /-

    Rules:

  • The individual speaker time is 10 minutes.
  • The discretion of switching the motion lies with the judges.
  • Preparation time of 1 minute will be given.

    • Judging criteria:

  • Must be coherent.
  • Proper use of Grammar and Humor.
  • Control over Vocal pace, Tone, and Volume.
  • Vulgarity in speech would not be tolerated.
