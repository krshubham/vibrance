About Us

After the success of the Chennai campus edition of VIT University’s cultural knowledge carnival known as Vibrance’15, it gives us immense pride and pleasure in presenting to you the 2nd edition of the same.

VIT University, Chennai campus, is now building on the huge leap it took last year, by hosting the 2nd edition its national cultural festival, from 18th to 19th of March, 2016. Vibrance’15 saw an enthusiastic and overwhelming participation last year with a large number of students arriving from different parts of the country to take part. Vibrance’16 will be no exception and the number of participants is only expected to scale greater heights this time.

This carnival will be filled with numerous activities such as dance battles, musical fiestas, etc; organized by the various schools of the University with the primary objective being - “to explore new talents and impart the qualities to the students”.

The cultural extravaganza also comprises of sports events which is expected to thrill and create an atmosphere of fun and excitement to the participating students.

This phenomenal event comprises of a new addition to it this year, The Pro-Show. Pro- Shows are a huge part of any cultural festival. We at the University promises its audience an atmosphere of immense pleasure and satisfaction leaving them wanting for more.

In addition to the home crowd, an enormous number of participants from over 100 engineering colleges and many universities in and around Chennai and other parts of the country are expected to take part.
