Code of conduct

  • Discipline is to be maintained at all costs within campus borders.
  • Proper costumes/clothes are mandatory for everyone entering campus.
    They must be in accordance with university rules and regulations.
  • Vulgarity or indecency of any form, verbal or non-verbal, during events or otherwise, will not be tolerated.
  • All regulations for various events will be given during the event and are to be followed without exemptions.
  • Aggression toward people on stage will be considered as a criminal act and is subjected to penalty.
